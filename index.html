Project Description

The Baxter Creek Watershed Alliance (BCWA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to the conservation of the Baxter Creek sub-watershed in Peterborough. Working in collaboration with Fleming College, Esri software was used to develop user-friendly GIS applications and maps for data collection and visualization. An ArcGIS StoryMap, map series, two surveys, and a watershed characterization dashboard were created. These initiatives serve to enhance understanding of the ecological importance of the watershed, educate the public about watershed health, and empower individuals to actively contribute to conservation efforts. Through this collaboration, the BCWA and Fleming College have established an innovative and effective approach to watershed management, supporting a sustainable future for the Baxter Creek and its surrounding ecosystem.

Throughout the collaborative project, the products created foster a greater understanding of the Baxter Creek sub-watershed's ecological significance, educates the public about watershed health, and empowers individuals to contribute to the conservation efforts. By using the power of GIS technology and knowledge of its functionality, the BCWA and Fleming College were able to establish an innovative and effective approach to watershed management, ensuring a sustainable future for the Baxter Creek and its surrounding ecosystem.
